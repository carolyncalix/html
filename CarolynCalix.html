All About Me!

I'm Carolyn Daniela Calix I was born September 16, 1999 in Miami. I have a very big family. I have six siblings; three sisters and three brothers. My mom and dad are together and have been married for 25 years.

My parents and my older siblings are from Honduras. I was the first one in my family to be born in the United States. My mom came pregnant with me. The plan was to leave once I was born but my parents decied to stay. My dad at first worked in roofing for some years and saved up a lot of what he made to open his first business here. He now has now open mulitple businesses.

I have lived in Miami, Florida my whole life. I went to Jack D. Gordon Elementry School, Jorge Mas Canosa Middle School, and I currently attend Coral Reef High School. I plan to attend Florida International University and study marketing and finance.
